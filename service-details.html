Income Tax

At MS Tax & Accounting Service, our seasoned experts are dedicated to simplifying the income tax filing process for you. With an in-depth understanding of the latest tax laws, we ensure full compliance while strategically maximizing your deductions. Our team diligently works to identify every opportunity for tax savings, reducing your liabilities and enhancing your returns. Whether you are an individual or a business, our personalized approach means you get tailored tax solutions that reflect your unique financial situation.

Let us handle the complexities of tax filing so you can focus on what matters most to you.

Bookkeeping:

At MS Tax & Accounting Service, we understand the importance of meticulous financial record-keeping, especially for small and medium-sized businesses. Our precise bookkeeping services are tailored to maintain your accounts accurately, ensuring that every transaction is logged systematically. This not only helps in keeping your financial records in order but also supports effective financial planning and analysis.

By entrusting us with your bookkeeping, you gain peace of mind knowing that your financial data is organized and up-to-date, enabling you to make informed decisions and focus on growing your business.

New Business Startups

At MS Tax & Accounting Service, we specialize in equipping new businesses with the foundational knowledge they need to thrive. From choosing the right business structure to ensuring compliance with local and federal regulations, our expert advisors are here to guide you through every step. We provide strategic financial advice tailored to your unique business needs, helping you avoid common pitfalls and establish a robust framework for financial management.

Our goal is to set you on the path to success from day one, ensuring your new venture has the best possible start in today's competitive market.

Payroll and Sales Tax:

Our comprehensive payroll and sales tax services at MS Tax & Accounting Service are designed to streamline your business operations and ensure compliance with all regulatory requirements. We handle the complexities of payroll processing, including the calculation of wages, withholding taxes, and filing necessary paperwork. Additionally, we provide expert guidance on sales tax obligations, helping you understand and manage your tax liabilities in various jurisdictions.

Our goal is to keep your business running smoothly and efficiently, freeing you to focus on growth and profitability.

Federal and State Audits:

At MS Tax & Accounting Service, we understand the stress and complexities involved in facing federal and state audits. Our experienced professionals are equipped to offer you robust support and representation during these audits, ensuring that your filings are defended effectively. We work closely with you to prepare all necessary documentation and to develop strategies that minimize your exposure to potential liabilities.

Our proactive approach aims to make the audit process as smooth and stress-free as possible, giving you peace of mind and safeguarding your financial interests.

Offer in Compromise:

At MS Tax & Accounting Service, we specialize in negotiating with tax authorities to potentially reduce your tax burden through an Offer in Compromise. This service is designed for clients who may face financial difficulties or believe their tax liabilities exceed their ability to pay. Our skilled tax professionals will advocate on your behalf, exploring every avenue to argue for a reduced tax obligation based on your unique financial situation.

By effectively negotiating these offers, we strive to provide significant financial relief, making it easier for you to manage your tax responsibilities.

We tailor our services to match your individual financial goals, ensuring that every piece of advice we give and strategy we implement is aligned with your long-term objectives.

Our aim is to empower you with the knowledge and tools necessary to make informed financial decisions that foster stability and growth.
